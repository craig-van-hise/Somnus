# Product Requirements Document: Somnus

## 1. Executive Summary & Product Vision

Somnus is a mobile web application designed to provide a non-pharmacological, evidence-based auditory intervention to assist users with sleep induction and sleep maintenance. The platform is accessed directly through mobile web browsers, eliminating the friction of native app store downloads.

Using a real-time generative music engine, Somnus dynamically personalizes an audio stream to guide users from cognitive hyperarousal seamlessly into Slow-Wave Sleep (SWS). It achieves this by stabilizing the cortical environment without introducing dynamic or structural distractions.

## 2. Problem Statement

The current landscape of sleep audio products suffers from several physiological and psychological pitfalls:

* 
**Cognitive Hyperarousal:** Everyday stress, anxiety, and rumination actively prolong sleep onset latency (SOL).


* 
**Predictive Decoding:** Pre-recorded sleep tracks or looping soundscapes fail because the primary auditory cortex actively seeks out patterns. Once a loop is memorized, the brain enters an alert state of predictive decoding.


* 
**REM Suppression:** Continuous broadband exposure (like leaving white or pink noise on all night) inflicts a sensory load that cuts REM sleep by an average of 18.6 minutes.


* 
**Performance Anxiety:** Visible countdown timers in sleep apps trigger the Ascending Reticular Activating System (ARAS), creating acute alertness and pressuring the user.



## 3. Goals & Non-Goals

### Goals

* 
**Mitigate Hyperarousal:** Engage the psychological pleasure of music using familiar, highly consonant intervals (e.g., Major Pentatonic scales) as a gentle cognitive distraction.


* 
**Enforce Sympatho-Respiratory Coupling:** Downregulate the sympathetic nervous system by starting playback at a resting heart-rate tempo (up to 80 BPM) and systematically decelerating to 50 BPM.


* 
**Create Unpredictability:** Utilize a generative stochastic engine to ensure the brain cannot find a loop, ceasing predictive modeling and allowing the thalamic gate to close.


* 
**Protect Sleep Architecture:** Utilize a 3-Phase Lifecycle (Descent, Hold, Fade) that collapses into a low-frequency drone before imperceptibly fading out entirely, minimizing sensory load during deep sleep.



### Non-Goals (Out of Scope)

* Requiring users to download an application from an app store.


* Presenting complex music theory configurations or technical audio modules to the standard user.


* Keeping continuous audio playing throughout the entire night.



## 4. User Experience (UX) & Interface Requirements

The psychological interface of Somnus must prioritize a frictionless setup to avoid performance anxiety.

* **The Primary Interface:** The main screen features the Somnus branding, an "ENGINE STANDBY" indicator, a central Play button, and only two fluid sliders.
* **Session Duration Slider:** Maps linearly from 30 to 120 minutes, allowing the user to dictate the total runtime.
* **Current State Slider:** Maps from "Awake" to "Sleepy" to control the starting complexity of the audio.
* 
**Hidden Timers:** The app internally calculates the targeted Sleep Onset Latency (SOL) based on the "Current State" selection. This countdown must remain strictly hidden from the user to prevent time anxiety.


* **Advanced Control Isolation:** A manual sleep onset target slider must be completely isolated inside an advanced settings modal, accessible only via a hidden cog icon for power users and system testing.

## 5. Core User Stories

* 
**As a user seeking to sleep,** I want to adjust my "Current State" on a simple slider so the app automatically tailors the music's complexity without me having to guess the exact minutes it will take me to fall asleep.


* 
**As a user prone to anxiety,** I need the audio to be free of sudden transients, volume spikes, or high-pitched treble so that I am not startled awake.


* 
**As a user optimizing my sleep health,** I want the music to gently fade away to complete silence before the end of my session so my REM sleep is not disrupted.


* 
**As a developer or power user,** I need to access a hidden settings modal to manually override the sleep target timeline for testing purposes.



## 6. Success Metrics

* 
**System Stability:** The engine must maintain stable background playback with low battery consumption during extended use.


* 
**Acoustic Integrity:** Zero sudden volume spikes or transients that could trigger defensive cortical K-complex deflections.
