
# Product Requirements Prompt (PRP) — [Status Marker] ✅

## 1. Project Context & Objectives

The acoustic entrainment subsystem anchors the user to a 100 Hz binaural root. To prevent low-frequency masking and dissonant acoustic beating, the Layer I Generative Bass Drone must be silenced. Furthermore, the remaining Layer II (Harmonic) and Layer III (Melodic) generative synths must be strictly confined to the G Major Pentatonic scale, with their oscillators mathematically detuned by +35 cents to shift standard G tuning (~98 Hz) to a perfect 100 Hz root alignment.

## 2. Technical Decisions & Dependencies

* **UI Update:** Rename "Ch 5: Generative Drone" to "Ch 5: Generative Music" in `SettingsModal.jsx`.
* **Pitch Matrix Shift:** Update `pitchEngine.js` candidate arrays from C Major Pentatonic to G Major Pentatonic.
* **Tone.js Detuning:** Inject a `detune: 35` property into the `Tone.PolySynth` instantiations in `GenerativeAudioController.js`.
* **Drone Silencing:** Remove the explicit `triggerAttack` method that currently fires Layer 1 in the transport start sequence.
