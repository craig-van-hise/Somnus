The 2OA FDN Reverb AudioWorklet from Phase 4 is not receiving audio. You need to update the Phase 3 audio routing. Do not route Layer I, Layer II, or Layer III directly to Tone.Destination. Instead, you must route their outputs directly into the input of the Reverb AudioWorklet node, so that 100% of the audio graph passes through the Ambisonic/OBR pipeline before reaching the hardware speakers.
