# Product Requirements Prompt (PRP) — [Status Marker] 🔘

## 1. Project Context & Objectives
The user has requested to lower the harmonic foundation of the binaural entrainment drones from Octave 3 (C3-B3) down to Octave 2 (C2-B2) to create a deeper, warmer acoustic bed. This PRP will update the global `TONE_KEY_MAP` to utilize Octave 2 frequencies and MIDI numbers, while simultaneously adjusting the generative music loops to maintain their current higher-register placement (Octaves 3 and 4) to prevent low-end frequency masking.

## 2. Technical Decisions & Dependencies
* **Octave Shift Math:** Frequencies in `TONE_KEY_MAP` will be halved, and `rootMidi` values will be reduced by 12 (e.g., standard C2 is MIDI 36 at ~65.41 Hz).
* **Generative Separation:** The `harmonicLoop` will offset by `+12` (shifting it back to Octave 3), and the `melodicLoop` will offset by `+24` (shifting it back to Octave 4).
